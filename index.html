The Story

Started by a 16 year old from Sweden

My name is Adian. I'm 16 and I started CoreX because I was tired of the fitness industry.

Brands charging ridiculous prices just because an influencer wore it. Or cheap alternatives that fall apart after three washes.

I couldn't afford the premium brands, and I wasn't settling for low quality. So I decided to do something about it.

CoreX is about making quality gear that real people can actually afford. No celebrity endorsements. No artificial hype. Just solid gym wear at honest prices.
